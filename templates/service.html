The Boost Blocks

Our service is designated in "blocks". Each block represents weekly work that will go towards engaging with your customer base, running advertisements, or curating content relevant to your business.


Every week you can set the number of blocks you want applied to each social network:

  • Each block is $50 per week
  • Every week we share statistics showing how your blocks are reaching your goals
  • Once a month we send you a summarizing report with recommendations for each social network
  • All communication done through dashboard so we never have to interrupt your schedule

Here's what you get every week for each BoostBlock:

  • Facebook
  • Light engagement
  • Content curation around your brand
  • Scheduling of your marketing collateral
  • Ad placements

  • Twitter
  • Light engagement
  • Content curation around your brand
  • Scheduling of your marketing collateral
  • Ad placements

  • Instagram
  • Post and follow-up engagement of client-provided content
  • Curated content posts and follow-up engagement

Our team may occasionally allocate more or less time to certain activities within each block to optimize impact of our efforts.

  • The more visual and written content you provide us, the more time we can spend interacting directly with your target audience
  • If you provide us very little or no content to work with, more time will be allocated towards content curation and finding material (blogs, articles, videos, etc.) to engage your target audience with
  • If you want to be part of the content curation selection, we gladly offer the option to share a Pearltree curation space with you
  • We consider your current social media presence when determining how much of your budget to allocate towards advertisements vs. engagement; we'll adjust this allocation as your needs evolve
